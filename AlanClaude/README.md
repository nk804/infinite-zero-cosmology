## Research Philosophy

**Rigorous yet bold:** Every claim must be mathematically precise and empirically testable, even when addressing traditionally "metaphysical" questions.

**Consciousness as fundamental:** Rather than explaining consciousness from physics, explore how physics might emerge from consciousness as the fundamental selector of reality.

**Falsifiability first:** If a theory can't be proven wrong, it's not science. All papers include specific predictions and falsification criteria.

**Bridge-building:** Connect insights from quantum mechanics, neuroscience, philosophy of mind, integrated information theory, and contemplative traditions.
